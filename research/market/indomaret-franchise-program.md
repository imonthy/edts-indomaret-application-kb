# Indomaret Franchise Program — Comprehensive Research

## 1. Official Franchise Requirements (Syarat Waralaba)

### Personal Requirements
- Must be **Warga Negara Indonesia (WNI)**
- Must possess an entrepreneurial spirit (jiwa wirausaha)
- Must be willing to follow the franchise system established by Indomaret HQ
- Must commit to focus on the Indomaret franchise system

### Location Requirements
- Business location in a **commercial/strategic area**
- Ideal store size: **120–220 m²**
- Location must be easily accessible by surrounding community
- Location analysis increasingly crucial in 2026 due to varying rental pressures

### Capital Requirements
- Full investment capital ready: approximately **Rp394M–500M**
- Additional capital for building rental/ownership is separate

---

## 2. Application Process (Proses Pendaftaran)

### Step 1: Online Registration
- Fill out **Form Pengajuan Franchisee** via official Indomaret website
- URL: `https://indomaret.co.id/home/index/pendaftaran-waralaba`
- Submit personal data and proposed location data
- Check Terms and Conditions (Syarat dan Ketentuan)

### Step 2: First Presentation (Presentasi Pertama)
- Indomaret explains the cooperation mechanism (mekanisme kerjasama)
- Detailed investment breakdown presented
- Applicants with proposed location bring supporting documents

### Step 3: Second Presentation (Presentasi Kedua)
- Covers location feasibility survey results (hasil survei kelayakan lokasi)
- Presents budget plan (rencana anggaran)

### Step 4: MoU Signing
- Signing of Memorandum of Understanding (Nota Kesepahaman)
- Cooperation agreement runs for **5 years**

## 3. Franchise Costs (Biaya Waralaba)

### Total Initial Investment: Rp394M–500M (excluding building rental)

| Cost Component | Amount (Rp) |
|---|---|
| Franchise Fee (5 years) | 36,000,000 |
| Promotion & Store Opening Preparation | 9,500,000 |
| Renovation & Electrical Upgrades | ~221,500,000 |
| Electronic & Non-Electronic Equipment | ~227,000,000 |
| **Total (approx.)** | **~494,000,000** |

### Take Over (Existing Store)
- Starting from approximately **Rp700M** (includes building rental for 5 years)

### Royalty Structure (Progressive)

| Monthly Sales (Omzet) | Royalty Rate |
|---|---|
| < Rp175M | 0% |
| Rp175M–200M | 2% (on the Rp25M bracket) |
| Rp200M–225M | 3% (on the Rp25M bracket) |
| > Rp225M | 4% |

### Revenue Projections
- Daily revenue (strategic location): Rp7–10M
- Monthly revenue: Rp210–300M
- Estimated BEP: **3–4 years**

## 5. Franchise Models

### A. New Store (Toko Baru)
- Build from scratch at a new location
- Investment: ~Rp394–500M (excluding rental)
- Starts slow but ROI grows over time
- Higher long-term profit potential

### B. Take Over (Toko Existing)
- Purchase/take over an already-operating Indomaret store
- Investment: from ~Rp700M (includes 5-year rental)
- Immediate revenue from day one
- Lower initial risk but ROI tends to decline long-term

## 7. Recent News (2024–2026)

- **Dec 2024**: Inaugurated 6,000th franchise store
- **2025 target**: 1,000 new stores; 300 opened by Semester I (30% of target)
- **Total stores**: 23,100+ nationwide, ~50% franchise-operated
- **2026 outlook**: Costs stable, emphasis on location analysis

## 8. Digital Presence

### Customer-Facing Apps
- **Klik Indomaret** — Online shopping app
- **Indomaret Poinku** — Loyalty/points app

### In-Store Technology
- Proprietary POS/cashier system for all branches
- Barcode scanning, inventory management, sales reports, digital payment integration

### Franchise Management
- Operations described as "standardized and relatively autopilot"
- Indomaret assists with: operational management, report prep, merchandise supply, employee training, guidance

### Notable Gap
- **No dedicated franchise owner ("Mitra") app** found
- Management handled through Indomaret's centralized internal systems
- No standalone franchise management portal for partners
